## Earthquakes

An app to visualise on a 3D Earth model (texture from NASA Visible Earth catalog) all earthquakes detected by the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) (https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/feed/v1.0/summary/all_day.geojson) in the past 24h along with historical ones (magnitude superior or equal to 8.5 since 1900).
